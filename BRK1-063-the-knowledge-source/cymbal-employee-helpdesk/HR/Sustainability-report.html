Sustainability Report 2025

Cymbal Retail is committed to a greener future.

Goal: Net Zero by 2030

We are ahead of schedule, having reached 80% renewable energy use in our Hubs.

Initiatives

  • Solar-Step Integration: 5% of all corporate sneakers are now recycled. Our flagship Solar-Step Sneakers SKU: CYM-SS-SOLAR are a primary component of this circular economy.
  • Green-Packaging: 100% biodegradable shipping materials for all "Quantum" line products.
  • Water-Neutrality: Our Munich Hub achieved water-neutral status in Q4 2025.
